Graph theory: Or how to become famous?

A century ago novelist E M Forster implored us to ‘only connect’. But nowadays that seems that’s all we ever do. But what if amongst all that clicking and swiping and liking there was some deeper structure or pattern. Patterns of connectedness have long fascinated mathematicians just like the rest of us. The field of mathematics which deals with connectedness is graph theory. But can this arcane realm teach us anything about the real world? Can it teach us how to become famous?
